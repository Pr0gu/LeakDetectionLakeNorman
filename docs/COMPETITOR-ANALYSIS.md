## LAKE NORMAN-SPECIFIC OPPORTUNITIES (unique market differentiators)

The Lake Norman market has characteristics no general plumber addresses well:

1. **Waterfront homes** — Dock plumbing, boathouse water lines, shoreline erosion affecting buried pipes
2. **Private wells** — Many Lake Norman homes are on well water. Well pump replacement, water quality testing
3. **Pool + spa prevalence** — Pool leak detection is a premium service here
4. **Second homes / vacation rentals** — Remote water monitoring, smart leak sensors, seasonal winterization
5. **Affluent communities** — The Peninsula, The Point, Connor Quay — premium service expectations
6. **Older lakefront cottages vs new builds** — Range of plumbing systems from 1950s to 2020s
7. **Hard well water** — Faster water heater failure, scale buildup

### Recommended new section: "Why Lake Norman Homes Need a Specialist"
Content pitch:
- Waterfront properties have unique plumbing challenges — buried shoreline lines, dock supply, boathouse fixtures
- Wells are common here (much of Lake Norman is on well water, not municipal)
- Pool + spa leak detection is a seasonal necessity
- Second homes need smart monitoring while you're away
- Salt air + hard water wears out water heaters 30% faster
